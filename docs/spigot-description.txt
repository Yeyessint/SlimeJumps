[SIZE=5][B][COLOR=#e5b53a]✦ What makes SlimeJumps unique[/COLOR][/B][/SIZE]
[LIST]
[*][B]Visual Route Editor[/B] [COLOR=#e5b53a][B](NEW)[/B][/COLOR] — edit flight routes in-game: waypoints appear as numbered floating markers and the whole path is drawn with particles. The wand adds a waypoint where you stand (left click) and removes the nearest one (right click). No other jump pad plugin has this!
[*][B]Trajectory Preview[/B] [COLOR=#e5b53a][B](NEW)[/B][/COLOR] — [ICODE]/sj preview <pad>[/ICODE] simulates real Minecraft physics and draws the exact launch parabola with particles, highlighting the landing spot. Tune your pads seeing where players will land.
[*][B]Charge Pads[/B] [COLOR=#e5b53a][B](NEW)[/B][/COLOR] — stand on the pad to fill a charge bar in your action bar (with a rising sound pitch). At 100% you're launched at full power; step off early for a partial launch.
[/LIST]

[SIZE=5][B][COLOR=#48b463]✦ Features[/COLOR][/B][/SIZE]
[LIST]
[*][B]Jump Pads[/B] — named pads on any block, each with its own horizontal & vertical launch power.
[*][B]Flight Routes[/B] — step on a pad and fly along waypoints to any destination, with a particle trail, whoosh sound, full damage immunity in flight and arrival effects.
[*][B]Double Jump[/B] — lobby-style mid-air boost on the jump key, compatible with /fly from other plugins.
[*][B]Holograms[/B] — floating text above pads using native 1.21 text displays. No hologram plugin needed.
[*][B]Editor Wand & GUI[/B] — create/remove pads with a click, or browse them all in a paginated menu with teleport.
[*][B]Presets[/B] — create pads with one command: parkour, cannon, bounce — or define your own in the config.
[*][B]Per-pad everything[/B] — power, direction (free or fixed), sound, particle, cooldown, charge, potion effect, action bar message and console command (rewards, server transfers…).
[*][B]PlaceholderAPI[/B] [COLOR=#e5b53a][B](NEW)[/B][/COLOR] — placeholders for scoreboards, tab and holograms (see below).
[*][B]Statistics[/B] — server-wide and per-player launch totals, and your most used pads.
[*][B]Safe by default[/B] — anti-spam cooldowns, fall damage protection, per-world disabling, sneak-to-bypass for staff.
[*][B]Bilingual[/B] — English & Spanish included; add any language by dropping in one file.
[*][B]Legacy friendly[/B] — pads from SlimeJumps 1.x are migrated automatically; optional classic "every slime block launches" mode.
[/LIST]

[SIZE=5][B][COLOR=#48b463]✦ Quick start[/COLOR][/B][/SIZE]
[LIST=1]
[*]Drop [B]SlimeJumps-x.x.x.jar[/B] into your [ICODE]plugins/[/ICODE] folder and restart.
[*]Stand on a block and run [ICODE]/sj create spawn[/ICODE] — that block is now a jump pad!
[*]Want a flight route? [ICODE]/sj route create togames[/ICODE], then [ICODE]/sj route edit togames[/ICODE] and add the waypoints visually with the wand ([ICODE]/sj wand[/ICODE]). Link it with [ICODE]/sj setroute spawn togames[/ICODE].
[*]Check the result with [ICODE]/sj preview spawn[/ICODE].
[/LIST]

[SIZE=5][B][COLOR=#48b463]✦ Commands[/COLOR][/B][/SIZE]
Main command: [ICODE]/slimejumps[/ICODE] (aliases [ICODE]/sj[/ICODE], [ICODE]/jumppads[/ICODE])
[CODE]
/sj create <name> [power] [vertical]   Create a pad where you stand
/sj create <name> --preset <preset>    Create a pad from a preset
/sj wand                               Editor wand (left click = create, right click = remove)
/sj preview <pad>                      Draw the launch trajectory with particles
/sj remove | rename | toggle <pad>     Manage pads
/sj list | info | tp | near | gui      Find & inspect pads
/sj stats                              Launch statistics
/sj setpower | setvertical <pad> <v>   Launch strength
/sj setdirection <pad> <look|here>     Free or fixed launch direction
/sj setsound | setparticle <pad> ...   Per-pad effects
/sj setcooldown <pad> <ms|default>     Per-pad cooldown
/sj setcharge <pad> <ms|off>           Turn a pad into a charge pad
/sj seteffect <pad> <effect> [s] [lvl] Potion effect on use
/sj setmessage <pad> <text|none>       Action bar message on use
/sj sethologram <pad> <text|none>      Floating hologram above the pad
/sj setcommand <pad> <command|none>    Console command on use (%player%)
/sj setroute <pad> <route|none>        Link a pad to a flight route
/sj route create|edit|addpoint|delpoint|remove|list|info
/sj reload                             Reload everything
[/CODE]

[SIZE=5][B][COLOR=#48b463]✦ PlaceholderAPI[/COLOR][/B][/SIZE]
[CODE]
%slimejumps_total_launches%    Server-wide launch count
%slimejumps_player_launches%   The player's launch count
%slimejumps_pads%              Number of registered pads
%slimejumps_routes%            Number of registered routes
%slimejumps_top_pad%           Name of the most used pad
%slimejumps_top_pad_uses%      Its launch count
[/CODE]

[/CODE]
